The Top 5 Most Essential Classic Rock Albums

Led Zeppelin IV



  • This album had no title, nor did it mention the band's name

  • The song "Black Dog" was named after a stay who used to visit the estate where they recorded this album

  • This album has sold 37 million copies worldwide

Sgt. Peppers Lonley Hearts Club Band



  • This album has earned the title of Greatest Album of all time, according to rolling stone magazine

  • 700 hours were spent recording this album

  • the lyrics to every song were fully printed on the back of the album, which had never been donebefore.

Dark Side of The Moon



  • This was Pink Floyd's first album to break the U.S. top 40 Charts

  • Thi record sold 40 million copies, and remains one of the best selling albums worldwide

  • This is a concept album, touching on the subjects of maddness and death

Black Sabbath



  • This album is known as the birth of Heavy Metal

  • The first song on the album, was inspired by the 1963 Boris Karloff movie titled: Black Sabbath

  • When this record was released, the record label included an upside down cross in the gatefold of the vinyl. When the band found out they were furious

Van Halen



  • This album was released in 1978

  • Eddie Van Halen, the lead guitarist of Van Halen, almost reinvented the guitar on this record. He invented a new "tapping" method.

  • Van Halen exploded ontp the scene, and blew everyones minds with the song "Eruption"
